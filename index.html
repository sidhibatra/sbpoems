About the Author

SB

Sidhi Batra

Welcome to my personal sanctuary of words. I'm Sidhi, a poet who finds solace in weaving emotions into verses. This space is my refuge—a place where I can share my innermost thoughts through poetry.

My poems explore themes of love, loss, nature, and self-discovery. Each piece is a fragment of my journey, an attempt to capture the ephemeral moments that define our human experience.

This website is my safe space—a digital home for my poetry where I can connect with readers who find meaning in my words. I welcome your thoughts and reflections in the comments, and I'll do my best to respond to each one.

Thank you for visiting and sharing this space with me.
